The Curry Wok

★ ★ ★ ★ ★
5.0 Overall Rating • Reviewed 6 June 2021

From Aromas to Indulgence - Every Bite Tells a Story

Kudla’s culinary scene just got a serious upgrade. The Curry Wok brings cosmopolitan sensibilities straight from the kitchens of Mumbai into Mangalore, transforming the high-dining landscape with its inventive fusion dishes. From the moment you step in, there’s an unmistakable air of precision, passion, and playfulness - a place where chefs push boundaries, and patrons are treated to both flair and finesse. If you’re ready to explore flavours that blend global inspiration with local charm, The Curry Wok is your next stop.

Here's What I Had


Bharwan Tandoori Potato - Rs 230

It’s rare for vegetarian dishes to arrive with this much thought and flair. The Bharwan (stuffed) Tandoori Potato is filled with a mix of mashed cottage cheese, mushrooms, and spinach. Each bite delivers a burst of aromatic spices that hits the palate with precision. Chunky, hearty, and perfectly spiced, this dish is one of the best starters here - ideal as a finger food or shared bite.


Baby Lamb Galawati - Rs 350

Legend says the Galawati Kabab was originally created for a nawab who had lost his teeth and it’s easy to see why. This baby lamb kebab is tenderized 18 times, giving it a melt-in-your-mouth texture that’s decadently soft. The spice mix - clove, bay leaf, cardamom, and star anise is aromatic without overpowering, while the flattened sheermal bread infused with saffron adds a subtle royal sweetness. Each mouthful lingers long after you’ve swallowed.


Goat Cheese Gnocchi - Rs 250

A little dumpling of joy! The gnocchi are steamed lightly and soft to the point of delicate mushiness. The filling - lightly salted goat cheese which pairs beautifully with the coarse, well-seasoned sauce they’re served in, creating a contrast of textures and flavours that feels indulgent yet refined.


Chicken Smoked Pot Tikka - Rs 295

Smoke, spice, and succulence define this dish. The pot cooking method locks in aromas, intensifying the flavour of the chilli-marinated chicken. The condensation beads on the meat’s surface release fragrance as you dig in, and the meat itself is tender, juicy, and deeply satisfying. A bold yet elegant dish.


Shallow Fried Corn Kernel Basket - Rs 230

A playful accompaniment, these crunchy kernels are fun to eat and perfectly presented. Light, crisp, and subtly spiced, this dish complements drinks or acts as a small appetizer to start a meal on a cheerful note.


Burrata Cheese Caponata - Rs 300

This salad is a showstopper. Crunchy romaine and iceberg lettuce, pine nuts, zesty cherry tomatoes, and mellow, stretchy burrata (mozzarella with a cream cheese filling) come together under a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and tangy balsamic dressing. Each bite balances freshness, creaminess, and acidity, making it one of the café’s


Teriyaki Chicken Pizza - Rs 350

Thin-crust perfection loaded with glazed teriyaki chicken chunks, minimal but well-placed vegetables, and gooey cheese. The combination of Japanese-style candied chicken with an Italian base results in a pizza that feels both indulgent and sophisticated - fusion done correctly, and delightfully so.


Kingfish Fillet With Singaporean Egg Sauce - APS

A curious but utterly satisfying dish. The mild kingfish fillet soaks up the creamy, savoury egg sauce, while sesame seeds sprinkled on top provide a subtle nutty crunch. It’s a delicate, balanced seafood creation that’s both innovative and comforting.


Tandoor Jheenga Peeli Mirch - APS

Fresh, juicy, and mildly seasoned prawns that let the natural sweetness of the crustacean shine through. These prawns stake a strong claim as “king of seafood” - simplicity meets perfection here.


Prawns XO Sauce - Rs 415

Over-the-top but in the best possible way. Batter-fried prawns served with a spicy, seafood-infused XO sauce. The sauce is intense, aromatic, and complex, perfectly complementing the crisp, succulent prawns. Each bite is a dramatic, flavour-packed moment that leaves a lasting impression.


Dudhi Halwa - Rs 150

A comforting end to a complex meal. While bottle gourd might not appeal to all, the halwa’s creamy caramelisation with cardamom seeds gives it a gentle, warming sweetness - a satisfying conclusion to a feast of bold, inventive flavours.


Final Thoughts

The Curry Wok is a destination for culinary enthusiasts who appreciate bold experimentation executed with finesse. From intricate vegetarian starters to indulgent seafood and innovative fusion mains, every dish showcases creativity, precision, and attention to detail. While some items surprise with unexpected flavour combinations, the balance and execution rarely disappoint. For those seeking Mangalore’s finest fusion dining, The Curry Wok is a masterclass in flavour, presentation, and gastronomic adventure - a place to savour slowly, bite by bite, and remember.
